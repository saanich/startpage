You are getting a new computer!

The Next Generation Desktop (NGD) project involves replacing old computers and outdated software with new computers that have up to date software. New computers have Windows 7 and Microsoft Office 2013.

If you do not see the answer to any questions you may have, please contact the IT Helpdesk

When is it happening?

The roll out starts with the pilot in the first week of March and then will continue for 4 months.

Some groups seek August/September delivery.

The schedule for delivering computers and providing training is coordinated with each section to minimize disruption and will be communicated directly to you as well as posted on this website

How are PCs delivered?

The new computers are delivered in a series of waves to different groups every day, according to the schedule, 10 computers per day. Training consultant, as well as IT staff will be available to provide support on site.

You will be directly communicated prior to the scheduled delivery. Your departmental representatives and “super users” will also be available to help.

On the day your computer arrives…

9:00 – 10:30

Mandatory training: Each staff member will be scheduled into a training class at the Learning Centre to cover the transition from Windows XP/Office 2003 to Windows 7 / Office 2013.

Computer installation: While in the class, your old computer will be removed and replaced with the equipment identified in your needs assessment.

10:30 – 11:30

Computer setup: When you return from training, you will log in and follow a short checklist to get started with your new computer.

Support

If an issue arises or you have questions, the following options are available:

  • IT staff will be available in your area on delivery day for about 90 minutes to assist
  • Each Division/Section has an NGD Rep to answer questions about the software and hardware that you will receive
  • A Windows/Office 2013 Training consultant is available on site up to 2 hrs/day.
  • IT Helpdesk is available anytime between 8:30 am, and 4:30 pm
